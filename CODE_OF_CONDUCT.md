# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/),
version 2.1, as its code of conduct.

In short: be respectful and constructive. We pledge to make participation a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity.
Harassment and abusive behavior are not tolerated.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to **soohoon@greptile.com**. All reports will be
reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly, and the privacy and security of
the reporter will be respected. The full text of the Contributor Covenant 2.1
is available at the link above.
